About Company Page


We at Fest Management System bridge the gap between the vendors and the fest organizer bodies


We are working since 5 years and have hosted many eminent personalities like Maroon 5,Eminem etc. We help the students to organize their fest smoothly. Our company is filled with hard-working and skilled employees,who helped us in leading the foundation of this company.We at FMS provide all the things needed to organize a fest. We excel in organizing proshows,managing logistics and providing the delicious food.

Our Values
