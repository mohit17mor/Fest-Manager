Their are two types of users: 
	1)Customer (mostly a college student organizing the fest)
	2)Vendor

Customers can add available contracts into thier carts or remove already chosen contracts. 

Contracts are of three types:
	1)Logistics
	2)Catering
	3)ProShows

Vendors dashboard will display all his/her commitments (ie. their contracts which have been selected).

Vendors can even create new contracts which will then be displayed on customers dashboard.
